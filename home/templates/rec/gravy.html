Mushroom Gravy Recipe

A spin on the standard Mushroom Pizza . Mushroom Gravy is a quick finger lickingly good and rich gravy made with mushrooms. Adding cashew powder and malai gives the gravy thickness and nice restaurant flavour so don't skip it. I used homemade kashmiri chilli powder.Add 1/2 tsp extra if you like spicy gravy.

TIMING

Preparation Time:15 mins

Cooking Time:30 mins

TOTAL TIME :45 mins

Servings 3 Person

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups mushrooms sliced,1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 big onion finely chopped,2 medium sized tomatoes pureed
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder,1 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder,1 tsp garam masala powder,1 tbsp cashew powder just powder it,1 tbsp malai from milk
  • 1 tsp kasoori methi,1 tbsp coriander leaves finely chopped
  • 1 cup water,1.5 tbsp oil, 1/4 inch cinnamon,1 no cardamom, small clove,1/2 tsp cumin seeds

Preparation

Heat oil in a pan - add items listed under 'to temper' let it splutter then add onion saute until transparent then add ginger garlic paste and required salt. Saute until golden then add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and coriander powder. Give a quick saute. Then add tomato puree cook until raw smell leaves and the mixture is thick and dry. Now add mushrooms. Give a quick saute then add water mix well. Cook covered for 2 mins. Now add cashewnut powder, malai and garam masala powder. Cook until oil floats on top. Finally garnish with kasoori methi and coriander leaves. Relish Mushroom Gravy!
